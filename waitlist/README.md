## üö® Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ‚ùå Mistake 1: Too many form fields
**Problem**: Asking for 15+ fields on initial signup
**Result**: <10% conversion, few signups
**Solution**: Use Option A (5 fields) for initial signup, then progressively profile with Option B

### ‚ùå Mistake 2: No follow-up
**Problem**: Collecting emails but not nurturing leads
**Result**: Cold leads, low conversion at launch
**Solution**: Use email sequence from `EMAIL_TEMPLATES.md` (10 emails over 8 weeks)

### ‚ùå Mistake 3: Treating all leads equally
**Problem**: Sending same emails to everyone regardless of interest
**Result**: High-priority leads feel ignored, low-priority leads annoyed
**Solution**: Use lead scoring to segment (high priority = personal outreach, low = automation)

### ‚ùå Mistake 4: No urgency or scarcity
**Problem**: "Join the waitlist" with no deadline or incentive
**Result**: Procrastination, low signup velocity
**Solution**: Add urgency (early bird expires), scarcity (limited beta spots), FOMO (social proof)

### ‚ùå Mistake 5: Forgetting mobile users
**Problem**: Forms and landing pages not mobile-optimized
**Result**: 60%+ bounce rate (most traffic is mobile)
**Solution**: Test all forms on mobile devices before launch

### ‚ùå Mistake 6: No social proof
**Problem**: "Join 0 businesses" or no testimonials
**Result**: Trust issues, hesitation to sign up
**Solution**: Update count regularly ("Join 500+ businesses"), add beta testimonials

### ‚ùå Mistake 7: Ignoring low-priority leads
**Problem**: Only focusing on high-priority, deleting others
**Result**: Missing future opportunities (companies grow, needs change)
**Solution**: Keep everyone on email list, re-qualify every 6 months
