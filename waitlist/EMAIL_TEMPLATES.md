## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- **Keep it short**: 40-50 characters (mobile-friendly)
- **Use personalization**: [Name], [Company Name]
- **Create urgency**: "Last chance", "Expires in 24 hours"
- **Ask questions**: "What's your biggest support challenge?"
- **Use emojis sparingly**: üöÄ üéâ ‚è∞ (1 per subject line max)

### Body Copy
- **Start with "Hi [Name]"**: Personalization increases open rates
- **Hook in first line**: State benefit or ask a question
- **Use short paragraphs**: 2-3 sentences max
- **One clear CTA**: Bold, button-style, repeated 2-3 times
- **P.S. works**: Add important info or urgency in P.S.

### Send Times (Nigerian Time - WAT)
- **Best**: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM
- **Good**: Monday 10 AM, Friday 9 AM
- **Avoid**: Weekends, before 8 AM, after 6 PM

### A/B Testing
Test these variables:
- Subject line (emoji vs. no emoji)
- CTA button text ("Get Started" vs. "Book Demo")
- Email length (short vs. detailed)
- Sender name (First name vs. Full name + title)

### Metrics to Track
- **Open rate**: Target 25-40%
- **Click-through rate**: Target 10-20%
- **Reply rate**: Target 2-5%
- **Unsubscribe rate**: Keep under 0.5%
