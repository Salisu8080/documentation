## Copy Optimization Tips

### Headlines:
- Keep under 10 words
- Focus on benefit, not feature
- Use power words: Transform, Instant, Automated, 24/7

### Subheadlines:
- Expand on headline with specific benefit
- Include quantifiable result (70% reduction, 80% faster)
- Keep to 1-2 sentences

### Body Copy:
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Lead with benefit, follow with feature
- Use bullet points for scannability
- Include social proof (testimonials, metrics)

### CTAs:
- Action-oriented verbs (Join, Start, Request, Get)
- Create urgency (Limited spots, Expires soon)
- Repeat 3-4 times per page (hero, middle, footer)
- Make buttons large and high-contrast

### A/B Testing:
Test these variables:
- Headline: Benefit-focused vs. Problem-focused
- CTA button text: "Join Waitlist" vs. "Get Early Access"
- Pricing: Monthly vs. Annual default
- Testimonial: Quote length (short vs. detailed)
- Form: Above fold vs. Bottom of page
