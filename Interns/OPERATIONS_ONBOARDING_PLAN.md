# SayPeace Operations Team: First 30 Days Onboarding Plan

**Goal:** Transform 8 individual interns into a cohesive, high-performing startup operations team.

---

## Week 1: Orientation & Culture (The "Download" Phase)
*Focus: Understanding WHO we are and WHAT we sell.*

**All Roles:**
*   **Day 1 (Welcome):** CEO Welcome Address. Vision & Mission deep dive.
*   **Day 2 (Product Bootcamp):** Full demo of ALL products.
    *   *AM Session:* PyLearn (B2C) - Create an account, complete a lesson.
    *   *PM Session:* AI-CMS/Journal System (B2B) - Walkthrough of the admin dashboard.
*   **Day 3 (Role Breakout):**
    *   *Rev Team:* Competitor Analysis workshop.
    *   *Ops Team:* Tool training.
    *   *Tech Team:* Codebase walkthrough.
*   **Day 4 (The Handbook):** Review Company Policies, Brand Guidelines, and Communication Protocols.
*   **Day 5 (First Social):** Team bonding activity. "One Thing I Learned" presentation.

**Deliverables by End of Week 1:**
*   [ ] All interns have working access to all tools.
*   [ ] Each intern has successfully used/tested the product relevant to their role.
*   [ ] "First Impressions" Report (Fresh eyes feedback on our products).

---

## Week 2: Deep Dive & Strategy (The "Setup" Phase)
*Focus: Setting up the pipelines and workflows.*

**Revenue Team (BizDev, Growth, Finance):**
*   **BizDev:** Build a list of 50 qualified University leads. Draft 3 email templates.
*   **Growth:** Create a Content Calendar for the next month. Set up the Ambassador Program structure.
*   **Finance:** Audit current expenses. List 10 potential grants to apply for.

**Ops & Product Team (PM, Success, Design):**
*   **PM:** Audit the current backlog. Sort tickets into "High", "Medium", "Low".
*   **Success:** Review the last 20 support tickets to identify common issues. Draft 5 FAQ articles.
*   **Design:** Audit current social media assets. Create templates for "New Feature" and "Testimonial" posts.

**Tech Team (Ops Lead, Maintainer):**
*   **Ops Lead:** Audit server security and backup schedules.
*   **Maintainer:** Fix 3 "Low Hanging Fruit" bugs (simple UI fixes) to get familiar with the deployment pipeline.

---

## Week 3: Execution Begins (The "Go Live" Phase)
*Focus: First real output.*

**Revenue Team:**
*   **BizDev:** Send first batch of 20 cold emails. Make 5 cold calls.
*   **Growth:** Post 3 pieces of content on socials. Launch the "Ambassador Search" campaign.
*   **Finance:** Draft the narrative section for the most promising Grant application.

**Ops & Product Team:**
*   **PM:** Run the first "Sprint Planning" meeting. Assign tasks to the Tech Team.
*   **Success:** Handle 100% of incoming support tickets (with CEO supervision).
*   **Design:** Produce assets for the Growth team's Ambassador campaign.

**Tech Team:**
*   **Ops Lead:** Set up adequate monitoring/alerting for downtime.
*   **Maintainer:** Ship the first "Feature" (minor improvement) to production.

---

## Week 4: Review & Autonomy (The "Fly" Phase)
*Focus: Evaluation and full speed.*

**All Roles:**
*   **30-Day Review:** 1-on-1 meeting with CEO.
    *   *What went well?*
    *   *Where are the blockers?*
    *   *Goals for Month 2 & 3.*

**Key Milestones to Hit by Day 30:**
*   **BizDev:** at least 2 demo meetings scheduled.
*   **Growth:** 10% increase in social engagement or 50 new PyLearn signups.
*   **Finance:** First Grant application submitted. Monthly Financial Report generated.
*   **PM:** 2-week Sprint successfully completed and deployed.
*   **Success:** User Knowledge Base updated with 5 new articles.
*   **Tech:** Stable production environment with 0 critical downtimes.

---

## Key Routines to Establish
*   **Daily Standup (9:00 AM):** 15 mins. "What did I do yesterday? What will I do today? Blockers?"
*   **Weekly All-Hands (Monday):** CEO sets the tone and priorities for the week.
*   **Weekly Demo Day (Friday):** Show off what was achieved (New code, New designs, New leads).
