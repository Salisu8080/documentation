# SayPeace Solutions: Intern Job Descriptions

**Application Deadline:** Rolling Basis
**Location:** Remote / Hybrid
**Duration:** 6 Months (with possibility of extensive)

---

## 1. Business Development Lead (B2B Focus)
**The Deal Closer**
A relentless, thick-skinned Business Development intern to spearhead the sales of our enterprise software products (Journal Management System & AI-CMS). Your job is to get our software into the hands of University Deans and Conference Organizers.

**Responsibilities:**
*   **Prospecting:** Identify universities, research institutes, and academic journals in Nigeria and West Africa that need digital transformation.
*   **Outreach:** Cold call, email, and LinkedIn message 20+ leads per week.
*   **Demos:** Schedule and conduct product demonstrations for potential clients (virtual or in-person).
*   **Proposals:** Draft commercial proposals and respond to Calls for Papers/Tenders.
*   **CRM:** Maintain a clean pipeline of leads and deal statuses.

**Requirements:**
*   Background in Business, IT, Marketing, or Communications.
*   Excellent spoken and written verbal communication skills (English).
*   Ability to handle rejection and persist.
*   Proficiency with PowerPoint/Google Slides.

---

## 2. Growth Marketing Lead (B2C Focus)
**The Hype Creator**
A digital native who speaks "Internet Culture" fluently. Your sole focus is getting students to sign up for **PyLearn**, our gamified coding platform.

**Responsibilities:**
*   **Campus Ambassadorship:** Recruit and manage student ambassadors at 5+ major universities.
*   **Social Media:** Create daily content (Tweets, Reels, TikToks) that makes coding look cool and accessible.
*   **Community:** Moderate the PyLearn WhatsApp groups, sparking daily conversations.
*   **Campaigns:** Run email marketing campaigns to convert free users to premium subscribers.
*   **Analytics:** Track user acquisition costs and conversion rates.

**Requirements:**
*   Active social media presence (Twitter/TikTok).
*   Experience with content creation tools (Canva, CapCut).
*   Understanding of basic digital marketing (SEO, Email Marketing).

---

## 3. Grant & Finance Analyst
**The Money Manager**
SayPeace has a history of winning tech grants (FbStart, etc.). We need someone to operationalize this success while keeping our books clean.

**Responsibilities:**
*   **Grant Writing:** Research 5+ relevant tech grants per month and draft winning applications.
*   **Bookkeeping:** Track all company expenses and income using accounting software.
*   **Invoicing:** Issue invoices to B2B clients and follow up on payments.
*   **Financial Reporting:** Prepare monthly financial statements for the CEO.
*   **Pricing:** Analyze SaaS pricing models and suggest optimizations.

**Requirements:**
*   Background in Accounting, Economics, or Finance.
*   Strong technical writing skills (crucial for grant applications).
*   Attention to detail and high integrity.

---

## 4. Product Manager (Intern)
**The Vision Translator**
You will stand between the CEO and the Technical Team. You ensure that the developers are building features that actually solve user problems.

**Responsibilities:**
*   **Roadmap:** Manage the product backlog for PyLearn and Amina AI.
*   **Specs:** Write detailed "User Stories" and feature specifications for developers.
*   **Prioritization:** Decide which bugs/features get tackled in each 2-week sprint.
*   **QA:** Test new features before they are released to ensure they match the requirements.
*   **User Research:** Interview users to understand their pain points.

**Requirements:**
*   Background in Computer Science (preferred) or Business.
*   Strong organizational skills.
*   Familiarity with Agile/Scrum methodologies.

---

## 5. Customer Success & Onboarding Specialist
**The Client Champion**
Selling the software is only the first step. You ensure our University clients actually *use* and *love* our products.

**Responsibilities:**
*   **Onboarding:** Conduct "Train the Trainer" sessions for new institutional clients.
*   **Support:** Manage the Helpdesk ticket system and resolve user queries within 24 hours.
*   **Documentation:** Create and update User Guides, FAQs, and Video Tutorials.
*   **Feedback:** Gather client feedback and pass it to the Product Manager.
*   **Retention:** Monitor usage metrics and intervene if a client stops using the platform.

**Requirements:**
*   Extreme patience and a "teacher" personality.
*   Good technical aptitude (learning software quickly).
*   Excellent problem-solving skills.

---

## 6. Brand & Creative Designer
**The Artist**
You are the guardian of the SayPeace visual identity. You ensure everything we put out looks premium, modern, and trustworthy.

**Responsibilities:**
*   **Marketing Assets:** Design social media graphics, ad banners, and email headers.
*   **UI/UX:** Design high-fidelity mockups (Figma) for new app features effectively efficiently.
*   **Print:** Design physical flyers, roll-up banners, and merchandise for events.
*   **Brand Standards:** Maintain and update the company brand guidelines.

**Requirements:**
*   Portfolio demonstrating Graphic Design and UI/UX basic skills.
*   Proficiency in Figma, Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator, canva.
*   Eye for typography and layout.

---

## 7. Technical Operations Lead
**The Keeper of the Code**
You are the most senior technical person on the intern team. You ensure our servers stay up and our code stays clean.

**Responsibilities:**
*   **DevOps:** Manage Cloud Run, and Database instances. Ensure 99.9% uptime.
*   **Code Review:** Review and merge code from the Maintainer. Enforce coding standards.
*   **Security:** Perform regular security audits and patch vulnerabilities.
*   **Architecture:** Make high-level technical decisions on API integrations and stack choices.

**Requirements:**
*   Strong Computer Science background.
*   Experience with Linux command line, Git, and Cloud platforms (GCP).
*   Experience with Python (FastAPI/Flask) and PHP.

---

## 8. Full-Stack Maintainer
**The Builder**
You are the hands-on coder who keeps the engine running. You fix bugs, add features, and polish the product.

**Responsibilities:**
*   **Development:** Write clean, efficient code in Python (Backend) and React/Next.js (Frontend).
*   **Maintenance:** Fix bugs reported by the Customer Success team.
*   **Legacy Support:** Perform minor updates on PHP-based legacy systems.
*   **optimization:** Optimize database queries and frontend load times.

**Requirements:**
*   Proficiency in Python and JavaScript.
*   Willingness to learn and work with PHP.
*   Git proficiency.
