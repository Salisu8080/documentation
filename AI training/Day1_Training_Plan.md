# Day 1 Training Plan: AI Foundations & Teaching Applications
**Theme:** Demystifying AI & Building Classroom Superpowers

## 🌅 Morning Session: AI Foundations & Prompt Engineering (09:00 - 13:00)

| Time | Activity | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **09:00 - 09:30** | **Arrival & Setup** | Ensure everyone has internet access and at least one AI tool open (ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude). |
| **09:30 - 09:45** | **🧊 ICE BREAKER 1** | **"Two Truths and an AI Lie" (with Kahoot!)**<br>1. **Kahoot Warm-up:** Run a quick 5-question Kahoot quiz on "AI Myths vs. Facts" to get energy up.<br>2. **Activity:** Ask participants to ask AI to "Write 3 interesting facts about a university lecturer in [Their Field], one of which is a lie."<br>**Goal:** Highlights AI's hallucination capabilities while gamifying the start. |
| **09:45 - 10:45** | **Core Concepts** | **The Mental Model:** AI as a prediction engine, not a knowledge base.<br>**Tool Landscape:** When to use what (ChatGPT vs. Claude vs. Perplexity).<br>*Activity:* "Explore & Compare" - Ask the same question to two different AIs.<br>**SayPeace Spotlight:** Briefly mention how **Amina AI** (our product) uses these same foundational LLMs to power customer support. |
| **10:45 - 11:30** | **The Framework** | **The 5-Component Prompt:** Role, Context, Task, Format, Constraints.<br>*Activity:* "Prompt Transformation" - Rewrite weak prompts ("Help me with research") into strong ones. |
| **11:30 - 11:45** | **☕ Tea Break** | |
| **11:45 - 12:45** | **Power Techniques** | **Deep Dive:** Few-Shot, Chain-of-Thought, and Role-Based prompting.<br>*Lab:* "Technique Practice" - Apply all 3 techniques to one teaching task and one research task. |
| **12:45 - 13:00** | **Q&A / Wrap-up** | Review the morning's key wins. Transition to afternoon application. |

## ☀️ Afternoon Session: AI for Teaching & Content Creation (15:00 - 17:00)

| Time | Activity | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **15:00 - 15:15** | **🧊 ICE BREAKER 2** | **"The Impossible Student" (Kahoot Edition)**<br>1. **Kahoot Quiz:** "Guess the Prompt" - Show an AI output and have them guess which prompt created it.<br>2. **Activity:** Ask AI to "Create a persona of a difficult student." Participants pair up to answer the "student" using AI for explanation.<br>**Goal:** Re-energize after the long break with gamified fun. |
| **15:15 - 16:00** | **Curriculum & Content** | **Lesson Planning & Slides:** Generating comprehensive lesson plans and slide outlines.<br>**SayPeace Spotlight:** Mention **PyLearn** as a platform that uses AI-assisted curriculum design for Python learning. |
| **16:00 - 16:45** | **Differentiation & Feedback** | **Multi-Level Explanations:** Explaining one concept to a 5-year-old vs. a PhD student.<br>**AI Feedback:** Drafting feedback on student work.<br>*Capstone:* Assemble a "Teaching Package" (Lesson Plan + Slides + Quiz). |
| **16:45 - 17:00** | **Day 1 Closing** | **Homework:** "Try one thing we built today in your real class prep." |
