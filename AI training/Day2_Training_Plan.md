# Day 2 Training Plan: Research, Ethics & Workflow
**Theme:** Rigor, Integrity & The Future of Work

## 🌅 Morning Session: AI for Research & Writing (09:00 - 13:00)

| Time | Activity | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **09:00 - 09:30** | **Arrival & Recap** | Quick review of Day 1. Ensure access to "Research-grade" tools (Elicit/Perplexity/Consensus). |
| **09:30 - 09:45** | **🧊 ICE BREAKER 3** | **"The Hallucination Hunt" (Kahoot Challenge)**<br>1. **Kahoot Quiz:** "Real or Fake Paper?" Show 5 citations (mix of real and AI-hallucinated). Participants guess which is real.<br>2. **Activity:** Ask AI to find a specific fake paper. Discuss the results.<br>**Goal:** Viscerally demonstrate why you *never* trust AI citations without verification. |
| **09:45 - 10:45** | **The Research Workflow** | **Discovery vs. Generation:** Using tools to *find* papers vs. *summarize* them.<br>**The Golden Rule:** "Never cite what you haven't read."<br>**SayPeace Spotlight:** Introduce **SayPeace Research Hub** as a platform designed to manage legitimate research workflows (journals/conferences). |
| **10:45 - 11:30** | **Literature Review** | **The Multi-Tool Approach:** Elicit for discovery, Claude for analysis.<br>*Activity:* Participants perform a rapid "mini lit review" on their topic. |
| **11:30 - 11:45** | **☕ Tea Break** | |
| **11:45 - 12:45** | **Academic Writing** | **Drafting vs. Editing:** AI as "Reviewer 2."<br>*Lab:* "Improve Your Abstract." Critique an abstract against high-impact journal standards. |
| **12:45 - 13:00** | **Q&A** | Wrap up the morning session. |
